Chiniot Faislabad(CFD) campus wins photography competition

 Happening date 29-Mar-2014
 Posted date 23-May-2014

Muaze Nakhez, Muhammad Hashim and Ahmed Tehsin from FAST-NU Chiniot- Faisalabad campus represented Fast Photography Society at LUMS Annual Photography Summit 2014 held at LUMS from March 28 to 29. Above 30 institutions participated in the competition. FPS won the light painting competition where participants have to take innovative pictures while drawing from light.

The summit was an opportunity for students from all over Pakistan, who wished to learn and develop their photographic skills. The event proved to be a platform for networking,interaction and learning not only from each other but also from other great names in the field of photography, through workshops and seminars.
