Dr. Kifayat Ullah Khan Alizai
Assistant Professor

Introduction

Kifayat is working as Assistant Professor in Department of Computer Science, National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences (FAST-NUCES), Islamabad, Pakistan. He worked as a Post-Doctoral Fellow in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Kyung Hee University, South Korea after completion of the PhD. His research interests include Database, Data Warehousing, Data Mining, Graph Summarization, Artificial Intelligence, Internet of Things, and Big Data. He has several publications in the form of patents, journals, and conferences of prestige, including some research works currently in progress/review. He is an active researcher and working on various research assignments in collaboration with researchers from Kyung Hee University, South Korea and Carnegie Melon University, Qatar.
