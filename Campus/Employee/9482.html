Dr. Nouman Azam
Assistant Professor

Introduction

Dr. Nouman Azam received his Ph.D in Computer Sceince from University of Regina. Prior to that, He completed his M.Sc. in Computer Software Engineering from National University of Sciences and Technology, Pakistan and Bachelors in Computer Sciences from National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences, Pakistan in 2007 and 2005 respectively.

His research interests include game theory, rough sets, conflict analysis and group decision making. He is author of over 20 scientific articles, conference papers and book chapters. He is a member international rough sets society.

Dr. Nouman received many national and international awards including HEC best research paper award 2016 (Pakistan), Shen-Kuo research exchange program (China), Dean’s scholarship (Canada), Gerhard Herzberg scholarship (Canada), Verna Martin Scholarship (Canada) and many others.
