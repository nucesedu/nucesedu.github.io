Dr. Ejaz Ahmed
Associate Professor

Introduction

Dr. Ejaz Ahmed is working as a faculty member in Computer Science department in National University of Computer and Emerging Sciences (NUCES-FAST), Islamabad. He is qualified PhD from Bedfordshire University UK in 2011. He has his MS in Computer Science from the Coventry University, UK in 1995. Prior to joining NUCES-FAST, he was working as Assistant Professor in KFUPM Saudi Arabia. He has extensive experience in IT industry (software development) worked in Canada, UK and Saudi Arabia. His areas of research are Databases, Grid Computing, Cloud Computing, Information Systems and Software Applications Architecture. He is an author of two technical books on Oracle published in 1998-1999. He has an extensive experience in industrial, research based projects along with Journals/ Conferences publications. He has an excellent amount of experience on developing/ implementing many commercial software applications (ERP-Oracle/SAP) for his software development in Financial & Taxation, Oil Explorations, Health Care and Pharmaceutical domains. Being Project Manager, he has completed and go-live project of Student Academic Record System (NOVUS) for the university in a short challenging duration.
